Create Your Flashcards

  1. Open a new Google Spreadsheet.
  2. Create 2 titles: front and back
  3. Fill the rows with your flashcard content
  4. Click on "Share"
  5. Select General Access > Anyone with the link
  6. Copy just the id or link
  7. Paste the id or link below
Tip: Make sure your spreadsheet is shared with "Anyone with the link" to use it for flashcards.
